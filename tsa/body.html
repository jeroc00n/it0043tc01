Nagara

Warp/Escape

Nagara (長良) is one of the main characters in Sonny Boy series. He is a third-year junior high school student. Nagara has a long-black wavy hair with brown eyes. He is usually seen wearing his school's uniform, a white polo and a blue neck tie. Nagara is bored with the environment around him and feels like giving up on life. He has trouble communicating with his family and the students at school keep their distance and watch him with a cold attitude.

Nozomi

Compass

Nozomi (希) is one of the main characters in Sonny Boy series. She is a transfer student who has just returned from Berlin. On her first day, Nagara witnesses her tearing up a textbook on the roof. Nozomi has a strong will and never bends herself, no matter how difficult the situation is.

Mizuho

Nyamazon

Koyama Mizuho (小山 瑞穂) is one of the main characters in Sonny Boy series. She is a third-year junior high school student who always carries a tote bag with her three cats: Tora, Gen, and Sakura, which were her grandmother's treasured possessions. Mizuho does things at her own pace, and is a moody and frank girl who does not actively get involved with the people around her, often holing up in a secluded place strewn with the products from her powers.

Hoshi

Hope/Materialize

Hoshi (明星) is one of the secondary characters in the Sonny Boy series. Hoshi has long, black hair and blue eyes. Standing at slightly below 150 cm, he is amongst the shorter students. He has a star-shaped birthmark under his left eye. Hoshi is typically seen with an overly friendly, slightly unnatural grin on his face. Hoshi is one of the members of the Student Council alongside Pony and Cap.

Rajdhani

Pocket Computer

Rajdhani (राजधानी)(রাজধানী in Bangla) is one of the characters in Sonny Boy series. He's best out of everyone in matters concerning science and technology. Rajdhani is a rational, scientific and decisive in nature. He can get along with most people and he briefs everyone on his findings openly. He's close to Nozomi, Nagara, Mizuho, and Asakaze.
